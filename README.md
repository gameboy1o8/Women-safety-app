# **Women Safety App**

The **Women Safety App** is a mobile/web application designed to enhance safety and provide immediate help to women in emergency situations. The app enables users to send an **SOS alert** by pressing the **power/volume button** three times, automatically notifying their **emergency contacts** with a **live location**.

---

## **Features**

- **SOS Alert:** Press the phone's **power/volume button** three times to trigger an emergency alert.
- **Live Location Sharing:** Automatically sends the user's real-time location to emergency contacts.
- **Customizable Emergency Contacts:** Users can set up multiple emergency contacts (family members, helplines, police).
- **Simple & Intuitive Interface:** The app is designed for ease of use, especially in critical situations.
